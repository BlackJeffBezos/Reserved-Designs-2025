What is Engineering Drawing?

An engineering drawing is a precise, standardized technical illustration that communicates how a part, product or system should be manufactured or assembled. Unlike artistic sketches, engineering drawings follow strict conventions (dimensions, tolerances, symbols, scales and notations) that ensure clarity between designers, engineers and manufacturers.

When combined with CAD (Computer-Aided Design), engineering drawings become highly accurate, scalable and ready for production, whether for CNC machining, 3D printing or mass manufacturing.
