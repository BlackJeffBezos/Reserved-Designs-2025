Types of Investments

  • Royalty-Based Investment: Ideal for those seeking passive involvement in our production scaling.
  • Stock Purchase for Resale: Perfect for entrepreneurial clients looking to distribute our products in targeted markets.
  • Equity Partnership Investment: A deeper commitment for strategic investors interested in long-term company growth.

Royalty-Based Investment

This model enables investors to fund specific production runs or expansions in exchange for royalties tied directly to units sold. By committing capital upfront, you help us ramp up manufacturing capacity, thereby increasing our market penetration and your proportional earnings. Returns are calculated as a fixed royalty percentage negotiable based on investment size, applied to the net revenue from each qualifying unit sold over a predefined period, often 12-24 months or until the investment is recouped plus a minimum return threshold.

How It Works: Upon agreement, your investment is allocated to a designated product line or batch. We provide quarterly transparency reports detailing production volumes, sales figures, and accrued royalties via a secure investor portal. Once units are sold, royalties accrue and are disbursed monthly once a minimum threshold is met.

How to Participate: Submit an investment proposal via our contact form, including the desired amount and preferred product focus. Our team reviews and structures a customized agreement within 7-10 business days. Legal documentation, including non-disclosure and royalty terms, is prepared for e-signature.

Return Basis: Returns are performance-driven, scaling with sales success. Risks include market fluctuations, mitigated by our diversified portfolio and sales guarantees where applicable.

Stock Purchase for Resale

Aimed at resellers and market-savvy individuals, this option allows you to acquire bulk stock at discounted wholesale rates, enabling 100% ownership and profit from sales in your chosen location or channel. It's particularly advantageous for those with established networks, as it doubles as grassroots marketing support for our brand while granting you full resale autonomy.

How It Works: Purchase predefined stock units at a tiered pricing structure—deeper discounts for larger volumes. You handle distribution and sales, retaining all margins above our wholesale price. We provide marketing collateral and support to streamline your efforts.

How to Participate: Identify your target market and submit a resale plan through our contact form. Approval is granted based on location viability and alignment with our values. Stock is shipped within 14 days of payment, with flexible payment terms for verified partners.

Return Basis: Profits are immediate and uncapped, based on your pricing and sales velocity. Success depends on your market execution, with our quality assurance ensuring high customer satisfaction and repeat business.

Equity Partnership Investment

For visionary investors, this offers a stake in our company's equity, providing dividends and potential capital appreciation as we scale. It's suited for those who believe in our long-term vision and wish to influence strategic directions through advisory input.

How It Works: Investments start at a certain amount, granting proportional equity shares. You receive annual dividends and voting rights on major decisions. Exit options include buyback clauses after some years.

How to Participate: Engage our investment relations team for a due diligence package, including financials and growth projections. A term sheet is issued post-review, followed by legal closing.

Return Basis: Tied to overall company performance, with returns from dividends and valuation growth. Historical benchmarks suggest 15-25% annualized returns in similar design firms, though subject to market conditions.

Investing with us isn't just financial—it's a partnership in purposeful design. Contact us to discuss how these opportunities align with your goals.

White Labelling

We offer white labelling of our ready-designed products with your logo and marketing materials. We can also craft full branding if you’d like to start your own company using our products. see our branding packages.

The White Labelling Process: A Step-by-Step Guide

Our process is meticulously structured to guarantee clarity, efficiency, and mutual satisfaction. Designed with industry best practices in mind, it minimizes revisions and maximizes value, typically spanning 4-6 weeks from initiation to delivery. Below, we break it down into key phases for transparency.

Phase 1: Initial Consultation and Agreement

The journey begins with a comprehensive discovery call where we assess your branding vision, target audience, and product selection from our catalog. We discuss customization scope—ranging from simple logo overlays to full thematic integrations—and outline any bespoke branding elements you may require. An upfront fee, calibrated to the product's complexity, secures your slot and covers initial design adaptations. This fee is non-refundable but creditable against future production runs.

Phase 2: Design Integration and Approval

Once engaged, you provide industry-standard visual assets: high-resolution logos, color palettes, typography guidelines, and marketing collateral in formats like AI, EPS, or PNG (minimum 300 DPI). Our in-house design team expertly integrates these into the product's physical and digital mockups, ensuring aesthetic harmony and manufacturability. Within 10-14 business days, we deliver interactive 3D renders and physical prototypes (if requested, at additional cost) for your review. You have two revision rounds to refine, after which approval locks in the final design. Importantly, clients receive no digital design files or intellectual property beyond the branded visuals—our core product blueprints remain proprietary to protect innovation integrity.

Phase 3: Production and Quality Assurance

With designs confirmed, production commences at our state-of-the-art facilities. We manage sourcing, assembly, and rigorous quality checks, adhering to ISO-certified standards for durability and safety. A royalty structure—typically tracked via serialized batch reporting to ensure accurate attribution. This incentivizes ongoing partnership while allowing you full pricing control. Lead times vary by volume, with scalable options for just-in-time manufacturing.

Phase 4: Delivery, Branding, and Market Launch

Finished units are shipped globally via trusted logistics partners, complete with customizable packaging inserts. From here, the products are yours to brand and distribute as proprietary offerings—no affiliations to our company required. You retain 100% ownership of sales channels, pricing strategies, and post-sale support, free from liabilities tied to our Reserved Designs. We provide co-marketing guidelines to amplify your launch, such as shared case studies or affiliate promotions.

Legal Framework and Intellectual Property Considerations

Every white labelling engagement is underpinned by robust contracts to foster trust and compliance. Our standard White Labelling Agreement (WLA) delineates rights, obligations, and protections, reviewed by your legal counsel if desired. Key provisions include:

  • Limited Licensing: You receive an exclusive, non-transferable license to use our product designs solely for branded units produced through us. Reproduction or reverse-engineering is strictly prohibited.
  • IP Ownership: We retain all rights to the underlying product designs and patents; your rights extend only to the labelled units and associated marketing materials you supply.
  • Confidentiality and Non-Compete: Mutual NDAs safeguard sensitive information, with a 2-year non-compete on identical products in overlapping markets.
  • Indemnification: We warrant product quality; you assume liability for your branding and sales practices.
  • Termination and Royalties: Agreements are perpetual unless terminated with 90 days' notice, with royalties audited annually for transparency.

These terms ensure a balanced, low-risk collaboration, compliant with international standards like GDPR and UCC. We recommend engaging IP attorneys for complex scenarios, and we're happy to facilitate introductions.

Recognition

Our commitment to impactful design has garnered prestigious recognition across international platforms, affirming our prowess in addressing real-world challenges through empathetic, innovative solutions. A pivotal highlight was our participation in the seven-day Design Challenge at the University of Cape Town, organized by the Muizenberg Improvement District. This immersive initiative tasked multidisciplinary teams with enhancing safety and accessibility along the iconic Muizenberg Promenade, a vibrant coastal artery plagued by navigational hazards and petty crime. Our approach was rigorously human-centered: we embarked on extensive field research, conducting over 10 interviews with locals, tourists, and stakeholders to unearth pain points—from dimly lit pathways exacerbating vulnerabilities to insufficient signage fostering disorientation. These insights fueled empathetic mapping sessions back at UCT, where we synthesized data into actionable strategies, prototyping interactive lighting installations, community alert apps, and modular wayfinding sculptures that promoted inclusivity and deterrence without compromising the area's aesthetic charm. The challenge honed our mastery of design thinking principles, from divergent ideation and rapid prototyping to collaborative iteration and compelling presentations. Culminating in a showcase to industry leaders, our solution earned resounding acclaim, securing the "Most Innovative Urban Intervention" accolade and inspiring local policy reforms. This experience not only elevated our profile but also deepened our appreciation for context-driven design, yielding a documentary chronicle that captures the raw energy of grassroots innovation.

Complementing this triumph, our engagement in the Engineers Without Borders (EWB) Design Challenge marked another zenith, where we channeled our expertise toward equitable solutions for Australian Aboriginal communities. Focused on bridging critical sanitation disparities, our team immersed in cultural sensitivity training and on-site ethnographies to co-design with Indigenous elders and youth, revealing the profound psychological barriers imposed by outdated, undignified facilities in schools and remote residences. Eschewing superficial fixes, we engineered an advanced pit latrine system—dubbed the "Harmony Haven"—featuring bio-engineered waste conversion for odorless operation, solar-powered ventilation for privacy, and modular expansions for scalability. Infused with Aboriginal motifs co-created in workshops, it transformed a basic necessity into a symbol of dignity and autonomy, alleviating health risks while fostering mental well-being through user-centric ergonomics like child-friendly access and gender-segregated zones. Judges hailed it as a top contender for its holistic integration of technology, tradition, and sustainability, earning us the "Excellence in Community Empowerment" distinction and invitations to present at global sustainability forums. This endeavor profoundly enriched our solution development acumen, from culturally attuned prototyping to resilient material selection under resource constraints. Equally transformative was our deepened understanding of Australian Aboriginal cultures—their resilient storytelling traditions, environmental stewardship, and communal values—which now inform every project, ensuring designs honor lived experiences over imposed ideals. These recognitions collectively underscore our evolution as a design force, blending technical virtuosity with profound empathy to drive enduring change.
