Step 1: Protect It (But Not How You Think)

Forget the myth that you need a $15,000 patent before talking to anyone. Smart inventors use a $39 Provisional Patent Application (PPA) that gives you “patent pending” status for 12 months. That’s enough protection to start shopping the idea safely. Companies respect “patent pending” – it’s the magic phrase that turns “nice idea” into “we need to sign an NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) right now.”
